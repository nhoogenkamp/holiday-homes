About Us

Tucked away along the wild and beautiful Ballyteige Burrow coastline in County Wexford, our holiday homes offer more than just a place to stay, they offer a peaceful escape shaped by nature, tradition, and family values.

Over ten years ago, our family opened our doors to share this hidden corner of Ireland with guests. What started as a simple idea has grown into something special, thanks to the kind words and loyalty of visitors who return year after year.

We’ve created a place where you can slow down, breathe in the sea air, and feel at home. Our holiday homes are designed for comfort and calm, with features like full-size baths, walk-in rain showers, and stunning views of the dunes and the Celtic Sea

Every stay includes unlimited high-speed Wi-Fi, a full kitchen, and thoughtful extras like local toiletries and cosy bedding. Whether you're visiting as a couple, a family, or travelling solo, you’ll be welcomed with warmth and attention to detail.

Beyond our doorstep, the charm continues. Ballyteige Burrow offers miles of unspoiled beach and dunes, rich with birdlife, quiet walking trails, and unforgettable sea views. Within walking distance, you’ll find traditional Irish pubs, friendly restaurants, and activities for every pace, including horseback riding, golf, fishing, and a variety of water sports.

We believe in hospitality that feels human, not automated. Need a restaurant tip, a taxi to the ferry, or a last-minute birthday cake for a loved one? Just ask. Our family lives here too, and we’re always happy to help.

At Beachside Holiday Homes, we don't just rent houses, we share a way of life. We look forward to welcoming you, and helping you create your own seaside memories, one stay at a time.
